FIE Research Group


Our research focuses on investigating the mechanisms of Fisheries-Induced Evolution (FIE), modeling biological systems and experimental ecology. We alose explore the impacts of multiple stressors such as climate change and fishing on individual fish species, multiple species, and ecosystems.
